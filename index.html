EasyCopy Privacy Policy

Introduction

EasyCopy is a Chrome extension that allows users to copy text with a single click, even when selection is disabled or difficult on websites. This privacy policy explains how EasyCopy handles your data and privacy.

Summary: EasyCopy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

Data Collection

EasyCopy does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Store any user data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

All operations performed by EasyCopy happen locally in your browser and the data never leaves your device.

Permissions

EasyCopy requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • <all_urls>: Required to enable the copying functionality on all websites you visit. The extension only activates when explicitly enabled by the user through the popup interface.
  • activeTab: Required to interact with the content of the current webpage to identify text elements that can be copied.
  • clipboardWrite: Required to copy selected text to your clipboard when you click on text elements.

Third-Party Services

EasyCopy includes a link to Ko-fi for optional donations. Clicking this link will take you to Ko-fi's website, which has its own privacy policy and terms of service. We do not share any information with Ko-fi or any other third-party services.

Children's Privacy

EasyCopy does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will notify users through the Chrome Web Store update notes. We encourage you to periodically review this privacy policy for any changes.
